Welcome to Airline Manager 4

This guide is designed for new players who want to grow fast and efficiently. Follow these steps to build a strong fleet and manage your airline like a pro.

🛫 Getting Started

  • Start with the DC-9-10 – second best starter plane.
  • Next step: buy B727-100s.
  • Once you have a fleet of 10 x B727-100, you're making decent money.

📈 Fleet Expansion

  • Upgrade to MC-21-400 or B737-800.
  • Add many of them – anywhere from 30 to 150 units.
  • Then step up to A340-200 or B787-8 for mid-priced long-range routes.
Don't touch wages yet. Just keep expanding your fleet. Within 10–12 days, you'll earn enough training points to unlock the first page of Management Training.

💡 The Million Dollar Tip

  1. Complete Management Training.
  2. Issue pay cuts until you see: “Salary cannot be lowered if morale is below 50%”.
  3. Raise salary until morale reaches 100%.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you reach minimum salary.

Watch the tutorial on YouTube: Search “Million Dollar Tip AM4”.

✈️ Recommended Aircraft

Starters: Airbus A220-100, A320-200, DC-9-10, CRJ 1000, B727-100, C919, MD-88, B737-800, Vickers VC10

Mid-range: A340-200, Il96-400, MC-21-400, DC-10-10, B787-8, Max10

End-game: A350-1000, A330-800/900neo, A380, MD-11, DC10-30, B777-200, B787-10, B747-8

Cargo: A330-200F, A350F, B777-300ERSF, B747-8F

🏢 Hubs & Reputation

Owning 10 hubs gives you a permanent +9% reputation boost (from 45% to 54%) → more passengers per flight.

📣 Marketing & Eco

Always activate Marketing and Eco before launching your fleet in the morning – more passengers = more profit.

🏦 Avoid the Bank

Don't use the bank – no interest and they charge for withdrawals. Instead, invest in good aircraft, hubs, and hangars.
