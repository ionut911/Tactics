Achievements Tips

Combo Achievements

20,000 km + World Conquer

How: Create a route over 20,000 km

Tip: Fly anywhere with or without stopover

DC-3 + Greedy

How: Buy DC-3 and set cost index to 0 before departure

Tip: May unlock a third achievement if route is under 1650 km

Stopping Up + Any Achievement

How: Create a route with a stopover

Tip: Combine with Bermuda, Scandinavian Tour, etc.

Longest #1 + World Conquer

How: Singapore → Newark

Tip: Use stopover at London Heathrow if needed

Longest #2 + World Conquer

How: Auckland → Doha

Tip: Use stopover to increase distance

How: Singapore → Newark

Tip: Over 20,000 km with stopover

Longest #2 + 20,000 km

How: Auckland → Doha

Tip: Use stopover to reach required distance
